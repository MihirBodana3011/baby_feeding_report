Amul Taja ke baare mein Doctor ki Salah
ℹ️
Doctor ne Amul Taja (Cow's Milk) Ko Allow Kiya Hai — Par Kam Maatra Mein
Amul Taja pilana Doctor ke anumati se bilkul theek hai. Lekin quantity limited rakhein:
• Peene ke liye: Ek baar mein 100–150 ml se zyaada nahi — din mein 2–3 baar.
• Cooking mein: Kheer, dalia, oats banate waqt freely use kar sakte hain.
• Dhyan rakhen: Cow's milk mein Protein aur Minerals zyaada hote hain jo 9 mahine mein kidney ke liye heavy ho sakte hain — isliye quantity control zaroori hai.
• Boil karke, thanda hokar dein. Direct fridge se nahi dena.

Main Recipes — Step-by-Step
Sabse zyaada zaruri aur baar-baar banne waali recipes
1
Ragi ki Kheer
Breakfast | Iron + Calcium
Ingredients
2 tbsp Ragi flour1 cup Amul Taja1 tsp GheeKhaand (optional)
Method
  1. Ragi ko thode paani mein ghol lein
  2. Doodh ubaalein, mixture daalein
  3. 8–10 min dheemi aag par chalate pakaaein
  4. Geeli consistency rakhein, thanda karke dein
💡 Ragi mein cow's milk se bhi zyaada calcium hota hai

Kya Avoid Karein — Complete List
9 mahine mein yeh cheezein bilkul nahi deni chahiye
Cheez (Item) Kyon Nahi Dena Risk Level
Namak (Salt) Baby ki kidney immature hoti hai — extra sodium kidney par strain daalta hai. 1 saal tak bilkul nahi dena. High Risk
Cheeni / Sugar Teeth kharab karta hai, sweet addiction lagta hai. Natural fruits se hi meethas milna chahiye. High Risk
Shahed (Honey) Clostridium botulinum bacteria hota hai — 1 saal se chhhote bachchon mein Infant Botulism ka risk. Extremely High
Sabut Meva (Whole Nuts) Choking hazard — gale mein fas sakta hai. 5 saal tak sabut nuts nahi dene chahiye. Choking Risk
Gaay ka Doodh (as main drink in excess) Doctor ne Amul Taja allow kiya hai — par quantity limit zaroor rakhein (max 400–500 ml/day total). Zyaada dene se iron deficiency ho sakti hai. Moderate — Limit
Maida (Refined Flour) Koi nutrition nahi — gut mein stick karta hai. Whole grain hamesha better hai. Avoid
Packed / Processed Food Hidden salt, sugar aur preservatives hote hain. Biscuits, packaged khichdi mixes, baby food packets — sab avoid karein. High Risk
Teekha Masala (Spices) Baby ka digestive system develop ho raha hai — mirch, garam masala pet kharab kar sakta hai. Avoid
Packaged Juices Sugar aur preservatives bhare hote hain — natural fruit juice bhi 1 saal tak avoid karna chahiye. Puree dein, juice nahi. High Risk
Caffeinated Drinks (Tea/Coffee) Caffeine iron absorption block karta hai. Baby ko chaay-coffee bilkul nahi deni. Strictly No
Gaye ke Doodh mein Banee Biscuit / Market Khana Parle-G jaise biscuits mein sugar aur salt hote hain — sirf ghar ka bana khana dein. Avoid
Seedhaa Fridge ka Khana Thanda khana baby ke pet ko upset kar sakta hai. Hamesha room temperature ya halka garam karke dein. Important
Nimbu (Lemon) aur Khatte Phal Nimbu bahut acidic hota hai. 1 saal se chhote baccho ko seedha dene se unke muh ke aas-paas ya diaper area mein rash ho sakta hai. Thoda acidity aur pet mein jalan bhi kar sakta hai. Avoid — Acidity Risk

Important Tips — Feeding ke Waqt Dhyan Rakhen
🆕
New Food Rule (3-Day Rule)
Koi bhi naya food introduce karte waqt 3 din wait karein. Agar koi reaction nahi toh dusra naya food shuru karein.
🌡️
Temperature Check
Khana hamesha body temperature ya thoda garam dein. Thanda ya bahut garam bilkul nahi.
🥄
Portion Size
Shuruat 2–3 tbsp se karein. Baccha bhukha ho toh aur dein — force mat karein kabhi bhi.
🫒
Ghee Zaroor Daalein
Har solid meal mein 1 tsp ghee milaaein — brain development aur fat-soluble vitamins ke liye bahut zaruri.
🚨
Allergy Signs Dekhte Rahein
Rash, ulti, dast, aankhon mein sujan — yeh allergy ke signs hain. Naya food band karein, doctor ko call karein.
💧
Paani — Thoda Dein
Solid food shuru hone par din mein 30–60 ml saada paani diya ja sakta hai. Zyaada nahi — stomach bhar jaata hai.
🧹
Hygiene (Saafai)
Khana banane se pehle haath dhoyein. Kataai, bartan sab saaf hone chahiye. Baby ka khana fresh banakar dein — next meal ka rakhna avoid karein.
📈
Texture Badlein
9 mahine mein baccha thoda mota khana seekh sakta hai. Soft mash se shuruat karein, phir dheere dheere chunky banaaein.
🩺
Doctor Follow-up
Har 4–6 hafte mein weight aur development check karaaein. Khana kam khaaye ya weight na badhe toh turant doctor se milein.
Note: Yeh chart generic guidance ke liye hai. Apne bacche ke doctor ki salah hamesha sabse upar hai.
Koi bhi naya symptom ya concern ho toh turant Pediatrician se sampark karein.
